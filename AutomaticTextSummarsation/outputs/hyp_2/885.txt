britons is in the grip of an allergy epidemic , a report claims today
the number of allergy sufferers is soaring and almost half of those with severe allergies live in daily fear of suffering a potentially fatal reaction
a poll of more than 2,000 adults for the charity to mark the start of allergy awareness week reveals that over two thirds of adults ( 68 per cent ) would not know how to help if they saw someone suffering an allergic reaction around 21million britons have some kind of allergy , ranging from hayfever to reactions to food , medication and wasp stings
