a union plumber , a school teacher and a survivor of the 1999 columbine high school massacre were among the 19 women and five men chosen to serve as jurors in the death penalty trial of colorado theater shooter holmes
the 12 jurors and 12 alternates were chosen on tuesday in in arapahoe county district court after a selection process that began on january 20 , and which experts said was among the largest and most complicated in u.s. history
holmes is charged with shooting dead 12 people and wounding 70 others in the july 2012 attack at a century movie theater in aurora , colorado , during a midnight screening of the film the dark knight rises
