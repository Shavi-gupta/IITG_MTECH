delphi has completed the longest automated drive in the us , travelling from san francisco to new york city in the first coast - to - coast trip ever attempted by an automated vehicle
delphi , one of the largest suppliers of automotive electronics and safety systems , is using the long journey in the audi crossover to acquire real - world data from a sophisticated array of cameras , radar and lidar - a laser mapping technology - as well as wireless connected - vehicle technology
jeff owens , delphi 's chief technology officer , sees the automated driver assistance systems in the audi as building blocks on the road to fully self - driving cars in the next decade
