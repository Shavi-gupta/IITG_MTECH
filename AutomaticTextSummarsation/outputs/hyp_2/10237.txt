since herman melville penned his novel moby dick more than 150 years ago , scientists have been fascinated by the remarkable attributes of the sperm whale and its kin - the smaller pygmy and dwarf whales
now , fossils from an extinct pygmy sperm whale that died seven million years ago , have shed new light on the evolution of modern day whales
the new fossil is known from the well - preserved skulls of two individuals which were unearthed at a sea cliff along the caribbean coast of panama , shown on this map the fossils show that at one time small sperm whales measuring around seven feet ( two metres in length ) had a much larger spermaceti organ relative to their size
