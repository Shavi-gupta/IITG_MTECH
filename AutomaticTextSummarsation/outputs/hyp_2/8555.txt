while racism is thought to be rife in the southern u.s. states , a new ' hate map of america ' reveals prejudice to be common elsewhere
the map suggests that the coal region and rust belt in the u.s. , along with the south , have now become the most racist areas of the u.s.
the rust belt straddles the upper northeastern u.s. , the great lakes , and the midwest states , while the coal region is found in northeastern pennsylvania and the spine of the appalachian mountains
