a chilling new jihadi video has emerged on social media , showing a new militia of female jihadis firing machine guns and practising drills in syrian
the radical group of women boisterously shout religious slogans , praising islam and re-iterating their devotion to jihad the women appear to struggle with the weight of their machine guns and make a number of basic rifle mistakes
