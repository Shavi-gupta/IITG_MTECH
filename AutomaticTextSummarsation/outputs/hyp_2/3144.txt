the university of connecticut baseball team has welcomed its newest member : 5 - year - old hand of sturbridge , massachusetts
hand , who is battling leukemia , joins the huskies with the help of team impact , an organization that matches children with life - threatening and chronic illness to college sports teams
the courant reports seniors carson cross and blake davey were inspired to get involved with the boston - based organization after hearing positive stories from the university of connecticut men 's hockey team , which also welcomed a youngster to their team
